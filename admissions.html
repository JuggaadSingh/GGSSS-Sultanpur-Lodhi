Admissions

Admissions for the academic year **2025-26** are open.

Eligibility:

  • Class 6: Age 10+
  • For higher classes: As per transfer certificate and previous marks

Documents Required:

  • Birth Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhar Card of Student
  • 2 Passport size photographs

Note: Admission forms can be collected from the school office during working hours.
