About Us

Govt. Girls' Senior Secondary School was established in 1950 in Sultanpur Lodhi with the mission of providing quality education to every girl child. We focus on holistic development including academics, sports, and cultural activities.

Our vision is to nurture responsible citizens equipped with knowledge, values, and life skills.
